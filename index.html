To view instructions click on the ❔ icon found top right of the page.













Instructions




1- Please take a few seconds to read the phrase first

2- Click on the record button and start acting the phrase next to the microphone.

3- After finishing recording press on the stop button.

4- Click on the Download button to start a new phrase.

(Additionally you can listen to the recording before downloading to make sure that it captured the entire phrase.)
*Please note that the last recording is the one that gets downloaded*
